Animating Camera Movement

This demo animates the camera moving from the upper right, relatively far away, to right in front of the barn, fairly close, following a Bezier curve (that is curving in 3 dimensions). The camera is always looking at the center of the front of the barn. The keyboard callbacks are my usual ones:

  • 0 to reinitialize the animation
  • 1 to move one step
  • g to start the animation (go)
  • spc to stop the animation
