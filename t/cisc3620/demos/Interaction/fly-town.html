Town Forest

Moving the mouse while holding down "shift" allows you to smoothly look left/right from your position. Keyboard callbacks (wasd) allow you to move forward/back and strafe left/right. You can also rotate the camera left/right (around Vup) by 22.5 degrees (π/8) using "q" and "e". Note that you have to move the mouse; just holding down shift won't continue to rotate.
